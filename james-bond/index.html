The James Bond Series

Daniel Craig - Edition

Books

Ian Fleming

Casino Royale

Casino Royale (2006) follows James Bond, played by Daniel Craig, on his first mission as 007. He must defeat terrorist financier Le Chiffre in a high-stakes poker game. Along the way, Bond falls for Vesper Lynd, leading to betrayal and heartbreak, shaping him into the hardened agent we know.

Quantum of Solace

Quantum of Solace (2008) follows James Bond as he seeks revenge for Vesper Lynd's betrayal. Bond uncovers a secret organization called Quantum and teams up with Camille to stop their plot to control Bolivia's water supply, all while struggling with his own grief and anger.

Skyfall

Skyfall (2012) sees James Bond (Daniel Craig) return to action after an attack on MI6. Bond faces Raoul Silva, a former agent seeking revenge on M, head of MI6. As Bond unravels Silva's plan, he must confront his past and protect M, leading to a dramatic final showdown at his childhood home, Skyfall.

Spectre

Spectre (2015) follows James Bond as he uncovers the shadowy organization Spectre, led by Franz Oberhauser, who has a personal connection to him. Bond must stop their global threat while confronting his past and questioning his future as an agent.

No Time to Die

No Time to Die (2021) sees James Bond (Daniel Craig) pulled out of retirement to stop a new threat: a deadly bioweapon in the hands of villain Lyutsifer Safin. As Bond faces this global danger, he reconnects with old allies and Madeleine Swann, the woman he loves, while confronting personal sacrifices and the cost of his duty as 007.
